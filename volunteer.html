Volunteer Opportunities

We offer various volunteer roles to match your skills, interests, and availability

Education Tutor

On-site
5-10 hours/week

Provide academic support to children and youth in subjects like English, Math, and Science.

  • Teaching or tutoring experience
  • Patience and good communication
  • Background check required

Mental Health Support

Flexible
3-8 hours/week

Provide emotional support and facilitate wellness activities for community members.

  • Counseling or psychology background
  • Active listening skills
  • Cultural sensitivity training

Digital Skills Trainer

Remote/On-site
4-12 hours/week

Teach basic computer skills, coding, or digital literacy to youth and adults.

  • Technical proficiency
  • Teaching experience preferred
  • Patience with beginners
