Amina's Story

Amina, 16

Student & Tech Learner

Joined: 6 months ago

From Refugee to Future Developer

Amina arrived at Mahama Camp with her family after fleeing conflict in her home country. Despite the challenges of displacement, she never lost her curiosity about technology. When HopeLink launched its digital literacy program, Amina was one of the first to enroll.

"Before HopeLink, I had never touched a computer. Now, I can code basic websites and apps. The instructors believed in me when I didn't believe in myself. They taught me that my background doesn't define my future."

Amina now helps teach basic computer skills to younger children in the camp and is working on a mobile app to help her community track food distribution schedules. Her dream is to study computer science at university and create technology solutions for refugee communities worldwide.

🏆 Achievements
  • • Completed advanced coding course
  • • Won youth innovation award
  • • Teaching assistant for digital literacy
🎯 Future Goals
  • • University scholarship for CS
  • • Launch community app
  • • Mentor more girls in tech

Jean's Story

Jean, 18

Youth Leader

Joined: 1 year ago

Finding Purpose in Leadership

Jean lost his parents during the conflict and arrived at Mahama Camp alone. Struggling with grief and isolation, he found HopeLink's youth leadership program. Through mentorship and training, he discovered his natural leadership abilities.

"HopeLink gave me more than skills - they gave me a family. I went from feeling hopeless to helping other young people find their voice. Now I organize community events and lead workshops on conflict resolution."

Jean now coordinates a peer support network for young men in the camp and represents youth voices in community meetings with camp administration. He plans to pursue social work to continue helping displaced communities.

🏆 Achievements
  • • Youth leadership certification
  • • Organized 5 community events
  • • Peer support group founder
🎯 Future Goals
  • • Social work degree
  • • Expand youth programs
  • • International advocacy
